- **Training & Testing:** Datasets are split into training sets (to build the model) and testing sets (to validate performance).
- **Algorithm Selection:** Options range from simple statistical methods like **linear regression** to complex **convolutional neural networks** (CNNs) for image or language processing.
- **Optimization:** Algorithms refine their predictions by minimizing an **error function** (e.g., accuracy or mean absolute error).

## Key Takeaways

- The fundamental jobs of machine learning are **classifying data** and **predicting future outcomes**.
- The final product is a **model file** that can be deployed to cloud services or local devices.
- **Python** is the primary language used by data scientists, supported by various frameworks that streamline development.
